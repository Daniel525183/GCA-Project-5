Welcome to Well Quest!

The goal of this game is to collect as much water from the wells as possible.
Above, you can see a 4 by 4 grid of wells, represented by charity: water's signature jerry can.
The central four wells are free at the beginning of the game.
They will fill up with water over time, and when they are full, you can click on them to collect water and score points.
The rest of the wells are blocked. You can restore to their former glory by clicking on them with enough points.
It costs 20 points to free a well.
What will you do?
Spend points on many wells to get a higher rate of water collection, or save up points before the time runs out?
It is up to you!
